# {Causal Impact Analysis of Email Marketing Campaigns

Estimate the **incremental impact of digital advertising campaigns** using causal inference techniques to determine whether marketing interventions truly drive customer conversions.

## Problem Statement

Traditional machine learning predicts **who is likely to convert**. This project answers a different question:

> **Did the advertising campaign cause the conversion?**

Using observational data, we estimate treatment effects and identify customers who are most likely to respond to marketing interventions.

## Objectives

- Estimate the causal impact of advertising campaigns
- Compare observational causal inference methods
- Identify customers with the highest incremental lift
- Optimize marketing targeting under budget constraints

## Methods

- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Propensity Score Matching (PSM)
- Inverse Probability Weighting (IPW)
- Double Machine Learning (DML)
- Uplift Modeling
- Policy Simulation & ROI Analysis

## Key Outcomes

- Estimated Average Treatment Effects (ATE) and Conditional Average Treatment Effects (CATE)
- Quantified advertising incrementality through counterfactual analysis
- Evaluated uplift-based targeting strategies
- Simulated marketing budget allocation policies
